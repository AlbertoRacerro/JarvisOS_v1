# BlueRev Research Radar v2

## P0 — prototype decisions

1. Coupled biomass, light, carbon, oxygen, temperature and volume model.
2. Identifiability-oriented experiment design.
3. Hydraulic and thermal models with fouling-sensitive parameters.
4. Local protection, interlocks, permissives, alarms and degraded operation.
5. Engineering-data lineage for sensors, experiments, spreadsheets and simulations.
6. FEM model selection, load paths, convergence and environmental aging.
7. Design-to-cost for replaceable tubes and Smart Joints.

## P1 — pilot capabilities

1. State estimation and biomass soft sensors.
2. Constraint-aware MPC after the regulatory layer is stable.
3. Fault detection and sensor-quality estimation.
4. Operational digital twin with state and parameter updating.
5. Condition-based cleaning and maintenance.
6. Probabilistic structural model updating and reduced-order models.
7. Dynamic techno-economic decision support.

## P2 — scale and fleet

1. Economic MPC under weather and biological uncertainty.
2. Semantic module interfaces inspired by modular-automation and asset-administration patterns.
3. Fleet-level evidence aggregation without hiding site-specific differences.
4. Risk-based maintenance and remaining-life estimates.
5. Modular downstream processing selected by product value and separation burden.
6. Real-options gates for irreversible capital decisions.

## Research rule

No state-of-the-art method is promoted because it is fashionable. Every frontier unit must state:

- the decision it could improve;
- the baseline it must beat;
- required measurements and computation;
- uncertainty and failure modes;
- implementation and lifecycle cost;
- the experiment that could falsify its value.

Recent papers, preprints and demonstrations are research signals. They are not BlueRev field validation and do not authorize deployment claims.
