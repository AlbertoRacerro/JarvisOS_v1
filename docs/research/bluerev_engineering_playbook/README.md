# BlueRev Engineering Playbook

This research slice replaces formula-centric corpus extraction with a permanent playbook of engineering capability.

The playbook stores:

- workflows and algorithms;
- method/model selection criteria and trade-offs;
- architecture patterns;
- diagnostic and fallback methods;
- didactic problem-solving advice;
- established, emerging and frontier directions;
- BlueRev-specific transfer actions.

It does not use isolated formulas, definitions, spreadsheet cells or exercise answers as primary records. Those remain live-retrieval material or temporary QA evidence.

## BlueRev biological-modeling boundary

The playbook includes a dedicated gap audit for modeling outdoor marine cultivation of *Nannochloropsis gaditana*.

The audit distinguishes the current generic modeling agenda from the species-specific knowledge still required for:

- internal-quota, acclimation and photosynthesis-growth model selection;
- light history, photoinhibition and day-night physiology;
- nutrient co-limitation and storage;
- carbonate chemistry, pH and carbon-concentrating mechanisms;
- oxygen production and supersaturation;
- temperature, salinity, respiration, death and recovery;
- hydrodynamic exposure history, shear, biofilm and contamination;
- measurements, identifiability, model discrimination and experimental design.

No unsupported *Nannochloropsis* parameter is promoted into the playbook. Missing evidence is retained as an explicit research requirement.

Documents:

- `BIOPROCESS_MODELING_GAP_AUDIT.md` — modeling gaps, fidelity ladder and research streams;
- `BIOPROCESS_RESEARCH_BACKLOG.md` — P0-P3 research ordering by decision value and evidence needs;
- `DEEP_RESEARCH_PROMPT_MICROALGAE_MODELING.md` — citation-rich research prompt for species-specific model selection, data, experiments and reproducible implementations.
