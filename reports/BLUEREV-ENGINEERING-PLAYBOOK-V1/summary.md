# BlueRev Engineering Playbook v1.1

## Direction

The project stores **engineering capability** rather than encyclopedic content.

Standard formulas, definitions and case-specific exercise results are not primary records. The playbook stores how to:

- choose between models, theories, procedures and architectures;
- identify assumptions and validity envelopes;
- execute workflows and algorithms;
- verify work and separate error sources;
- diagnose failure and select fallback paths;
- move from older practice to current and emerging practice;
- preserve future directions;
- transfer capabilities to BlueRev.
