# BlueRev Engineering Playbook v1

## Direction correction

The project now stores **engineering capability** rather than encyclopedic content.

Standard formulas, definitions and case-specific exercise results are not primary records. The playbook stores how to:

- frame decisions;
- choose methods and architectures;
- execute workflows;
- verify work;
- diagnose failure;
- move from older practice to current practice;
- preserve future directions;
- transfer capabilities to BlueRev.

## Delivered

- 96 playbooks;
- 12 domains;
- 8 entries per domain;
- 43 used sources;
- 63 established, 31 emerging and 2 frontier entries;
- all horizons permanently retained;
- 960 record-level automated assertions passed;
- 20 catalog-level automated checks passed;
- 0 automated failures;
- 0 formula-only records;
- 0 exercise-result records.

## Meaningful non-green gates

- independent domain review: NOT_RUN;
- standards-compliance review: NOT_RUN;
- BlueRev prototype validation: NOT_RUN;
- BlueRev field validation: NOT_RUN;
- industrial deployment validation: NOT_RUN.

## Decision

`READY_FOR_USE_AND_CONTINUOUS_EXPANSION` as an engineering guidance catalog.

`NOT_AUTHORIZED` as automatic design authority or certification basis.

The complete machine-readable and human-readable playbook remains in the external research package while the direction is reviewed.
